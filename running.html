Running

ocaps is available in JCenter and Maven Central.

Dependencies

To add ocaps to your project, add the following to build.sbt:

  • sbt
  • Maven
libraryDependencies += "com.tersesystems" %% "ocaps" % "1.0.0"

<dependency>
  <groupId>com.tersesystems</groupId>
  <artifactId>ocaps_2.12</artifactId>
  <version>1.0.0</version>
</dependency>
